Introduction

Taraxacum is a large genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae, which consists of species commonly known as Dandelions.They grows from taproots and produces several hollow, leafless flower stems  that are typically 5–40 cm,the stems can be tinted purplish.Plants have milky latex and the leaves are all basal; each flowering stem lacks bracts and has one single flower head.The leaves are 5–45 cm long and 1–10 cm wide, and are obovate in shape, with the bases gradually narrowing to the petiole .The fruits are called cypselae range in color from olive-green or olive-brown to straw-colored to grayish, they are oblanceoloid in shape and 2–3 mm long with slender beaks.(Taraxacum officinale)known as common dandelion is well known for its yellow flower heads that turn into round balls of many silver-tufted fruits that disperse in the wind. These balls are usually called "clocks"

Etymology

Taraxacum is derived from the Arabic word tarakhshagog for a bitter herb.The Latin specific epithet officinale refers to the Latin word for 'medicinal'

They are weedy pests but useful as well as they play important role in ecosystem asa well.In lawns for ornamental use , they nourishes ladubugs which in turn eats aphids and they aerate and add nutrient to soil.

2.) Cut flowers

The flower buds that have been cut from plant ,as it can be use for decorative purpose.typically in garlands,vase display.cut flowers can also be harvested from wild and for dandelion they wilt up too fast when kept in vase.

3.) Medicinal uses

To treat stomach problems,appendicits,also use as remedies for fever,diabetes,diarrhea. it is used as skin toner. Roots and leaves of this plant has been eaten for thousands of years as food and medicine to help treat anemia, scurvy, skin problems, blood disorders, and depression.Diuretic property of plant is used as traditional chinese medicine for approx.2000 years.Dandelion has been used in traditional medicine in Europe, North America,and China

4.) Health benefits

It is highly nutritious,contains potent antioxidants: beta carotene which protects against cell damage,polyphenol help to reduce inflammation. chicoric and chlorogenic acid helps to decrease blood sugar level in foods, dandelion used as salad greens and also in wines ,teas.The leaves are high in vitamins A and C, as well as iron.Dandelion can help to fight inflammation, boost immune system, act as an anti aging agent in skin care products and even treat type 2 diabetes, cancer, and HIV.

5.) Perfumes and food

Dandelions are harvested from the wild or grown on a small scale as a leaf vegetable. The leaves (called dandelion greens) can be eaten cooked or raw in various forms, such as in soup or salad.Raw leaves have a slightly bitter taste.Dandelion flowers can be used to make dandelion wine.In Silesia and other parts of Poland and the world, dandelion flowers are used to make a honey substitute syrup with added lemon,Ground roasted dandelion root can be used as a non-caffeinated coffee substitute.

Symbolizes

It symbolizes joy,endurance,determination,good wishes, hope and prosperity.It is also believed that Apollo, the Greek god of the sun and medicine, had the Dandelion as one of his medicinal healing plants.

Season to grow

It requires moist soil and sunlight to thrive mostly in Spring.The common dandelion grows in temperate regions of the world in lawns, and on roadsides, disturbed banks, shores of waterways, and other areas with moist soils.
